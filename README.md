# Retro Ping Pong Game
 
🏓  Your only goal is to try and get the ball past your opponent’s paddle.

## How To Play:
### First Player(Left Side):

🆆  Player 1 goes up.

🆂  Player 1 goes down.

### Second Player(Right Side):

⬆️  Player 2 goes up.

⬇️  Player 2 goes down.
